## Register

product

## Users

Celiac patients in Yemen and their caregivers — parents shopping for children, family members managing dietary needs for loved ones. Users are navigating a condition that demands constant vigilance about food safety. They may feel isolated, anxious about accidental gluten exposure, or overwhelmed by the complexity of maintaining a gluten-free diet in a market with limited options.

Their context: mobile-first, often in-store scanning products, or at home planning meals. The job to be done is simple — verify if food is safe, find trusted alternatives, and get on with life.

## Product Purpose

Jouda World is the digital companion for Jouda Store, Yemen's gluten-free retailer. It helps Celiac patients and their families:
- Verify food safety through AI-powered ingredient analysis (camera + text)
- Discover and purchase trusted gluten-free products
- Find safe recipes using verified ingredients
- Learn about Celiac health management

Success means users feel less alone, more confident, and safer in their daily food choices.

## Brand Personality

**Caring, honest, generous** — like a trusted family member who always looks out for you.

The tone is warm without being sentimental, direct without being clinical, reassuring without being patronising. The name "Jouda" (generosity) sets the expectation: this is a brand that gives — knowledge, safety, community — freely and earnestly.

## Anti-references

- Budget e-commerce (Temu/Shein style): aggressive sales banners, countdown timers, discount-heavy UI, urgency tricks, overwhelming density. Trust over urgency.
- Clinical/hospital sterility: medical accuracy is critical but the interface should never feel like a doctor's office or pharmaceutical leaflet.
- Standard SaaS dashboard patterns: hero-metric templates, glassmorphism as default, identical card grids, side-stripe borders.

## Design Principles

1. **Warmth over sterility** — Deliver health-critical information with a caring, human touch. Medical accuracy without clinical coldness.
2. **Trust through clarity** — Every interaction builds confidence. No dark patterns, no aggressive sales, no urgency tricks. The user's safety is worth more than a conversion.
3. **Companion, not utility** — The app feels like someone who cares, not a cold tool. Designed for people who may feel isolated by their condition. Celebrate small wins.
4. **Empower through simplicity** — Gluten-free living is already complicated. Jouda should be the simplest, calmest part of the user's day.
5. **Yemen-first, inclusive by design** — Arabic RTL, culturally appropriate for Yemeni users. Local context (names, locations, references) over global defaults.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA compliance as baseline
- Arabic RTL — all layouts, icons, and interactions reversed appropriately
- Large touch targets (min 44px) — mobile-first PWA, users may be shopping in-store one-handed
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` — some users with Celiac report brain fog, fatigue, or sensory sensitivity
- Sufficient color contrast — red/green verdict badges must remain distinguishable for colour-blind users
- Cairo font loaded from Bunny CDN for consistent Arabic rendering
